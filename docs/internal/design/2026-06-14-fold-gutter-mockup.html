The fold gutter — 2026-06-14 (rev. nothing-at-rest)
Folding moves out of the text and into a fixed-x left gutter, the Obsidian / VS Code pattern. Every fold control sits in one column at the same x, at every nesting depth. At rest the gutter is empty: the best-editor consensus, and the choice that keeps the quiet surface quiet. The panels are live: hover a directive block to reveal its chevron. Prose, headings, and list rows have no control, so their gutter cell stays empty.
1 · At rest (light) — clean margin even with many blocks; hover to reveal
Nothing at rest, on any block, at any count. Discoverability rests on hover plus a tooltip, the Ctrl+Shift+[ shortcut in the help sheet, and the always-visible folded state below. Hover any opener row to lift its chevron from zero to full.

3 · Caret inside (light) — the open container's chevron is forced down
When the caret sits inside a container, that container's chevron is forced full and points down, in the stronger active ink, so the author always sees which block they are editing. The rest of the gutter stays empty.
