Media 3b, the hero image, direction B (the prominent lead-image card)

A concept gets a hero image in frontmatter: image: { src: media:slug.hash, alt, caption }. The field lives in the editor's details slide-over, the right panel that holds the frontmatter fields. This direction pins the hero as a distinct, prominent card at the very top of the panel, above the Details eyebrow, set apart from the plain text fields below it. The point is prominence that matches the hero's weight on the page. One image serves both the page hero and the social card (og:image), so a small note carries that link. Alt is debt, not a save block. There is no placement role: the site template owns the hero layout (unlike the 3a inline figure). It reuses the 2b capture card, the picker, the alt radiogroup, and the resolved-thumbnail treatment. Two states are shown, empty and set, in both themes.

The alt control and the caption field sit under the preview. Replace and Remove ride the image itself. The site template decides whether to render the caption on the page.
