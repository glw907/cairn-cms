Media 3b, the hero image field (polished synthesis)

A concept carries a hero image in frontmatter as a nested object, image: { src, alt, caption }. The field lives in the editor's details slide-over at the weight of its sibling fields. At rest it is one row: a thumbnail, the name, an alt-status chip, and Edit, with the caption shown beneath as a read-only preview. Adding or editing opens the 2b chooser as a centered dialog, where a real 16:9 preview shows the crop that will lead the page and become the social card. One image serves both, so there is one thing to set. Alt is debt, never a save block, and decorative stays an explicit choice. The site template owns the hero layout on the page, so the field carries no placement role.

Light: the field at rest, among its siblings

Left, alt written: the row reads Described, the caption previews beneath it, and the field is one row tall like Description is one textarea tall. Right, alt still missing: the chip reads Needs alt in the warning ink, and the publish notice counts it. Neither blocks a save.

Light: the edit surface, where the 16:9 preview lives

Add or Edit opens the chooser dialog. After an image is picked or uploaded, the capture card shows it: a real 16:9 preview of the crop that will lead the page and stand in as the social card, the describe or decorative alt choice, and the caption. Replace and Remove are quiet text controls beneath the preview, never floated on the image. Confirming sets the field; nothing commits until you Save the entry.
