Getting started

Your first steps

From a blank site to your first published post

Three small steps. They tick off on their own as you do them, so you always know where you stand.

1 of 3 done
  1. Write your first post
    You have a post started. Open it any time to keep going.
    Done
  2. Publish it
    Publishing puts your post live on the site. You can change it and publish again any time.
    Go to Posts
  3. Create a page
    A page is for things that stay put, like About or Contact. A post is for news and updates.
    Go to Pages
These steps follow what is really on your site.
Formatting

How to format text

Type the characters on the left
Type this What it makes
  • ## Heading A heading
  • **bold** Bold text
  • *italic* Italic text
  • [text](url) A link
  • [[page-name]] A link to one of your pages
  • - item
    • A bulleted list
  • 1. item
    1. A numbered list
  • > quote A quote
  • `code` Inline code
This is always here. While you write, you can also open it from the editor with Ctrl /
