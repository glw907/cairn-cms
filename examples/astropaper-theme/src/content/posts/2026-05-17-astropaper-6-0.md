Version 6 is a from-scratch rewrite: Astro v6, Tailwind v4's CSS-first configuration, and a
typed `astro-paper.config.ts` replacing the old scattered constants file.

> The goal was never to add features for their own sake. It was to make the theme easier to
> read, easier to fork, and easier to keep clean months later.

The visible surface barely changed. That was the point: a rewrite a reader would not notice,
and a codebase a theme builder would.

This cairn port is itself a small proof of the same idea from the other direction: the same
visible surface, rebuilt on a different engine underneath.
